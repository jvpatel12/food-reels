## ‚ùì FAQ Section

**Q: How do I order food?**
A: Browse through food videos on your FoodReels feed. When you find something you like, tap "Visit Store" to see more details and place your order directly.

**Q: Do I need a subscription?**
A: No! FoodReels is completely free to use as a customer. You only pay when you order food from a partner.

**Q: How can I become a food partner?**
A: Sign up as a partner, complete your business profile, and start uploading food videos. It takes just 5 minutes!

**Q: How are videos moderated?**
A: All videos are reviewed by our team to ensure quality and authenticity. We remove videos that don't meet our community standards.

**Q: Can I track my order?**
A: Yes! Real-time order tracking is available for all purchases. You'll receive notifications at each step.
